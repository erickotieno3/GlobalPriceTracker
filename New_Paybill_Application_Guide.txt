HOW TO APPLY FOR A NEW PAYBILL NUMBER
==================================

Instead of trying to change the ownership/name of an existing paybill number (787878) that's registered to another entity (KACH COMM SOLUTIONS), a better approach is to apply for a brand new paybill number for Hyrise Crown. Here's how to do it:

FOR M-PESA (SAFARICOM):
-----------------------

1. REQUIREMENTS:
   - Business registration certificate for Hyrise Crown (Registration No. BN-EZC3Z67A)
   - KRA PIN certificate
   - ID copy of the business owner/authorized signatory
   - Recent passport photos of the business owner/authorized signatory
   - Business permit/trade license
   - Bank account details (National Bank Kisumu Kenya account 01521209171200)
   - Letter of application on company letterhead

2. APPLICATION PROCESS:
   a) Visit a Safaricom Shop or M-PESA Business Center
   b) Request the "Lipa Na M-PESA Business Request Form"
   c) Fill out the form with your business details
   d) Submit the form along with all required documents
   e) Pay the application fee (typically between KES 999 and KES 1,500)

3. APPROVAL PROCESS:
   - The application usually takes 5-7 business days for review
   - Once approved, you'll receive an SMS notification with your new paybill number
   - The system has been configured to work with the new paybill number 959595

4. SETTING UP:
   - Once you receive your new paybill number, update the NUMBER_PAYBILL constant in your code
   - Test the new paybill number with small transactions
   - Update all your marketing materials and communications with the new number

FOR AIRTEL MONEY:
-----------------

1. REQUIREMENTS:
   - Similar to M-PESA requirements
   - Business registration documents
   - KRA PIN
   - ID of directors/owners
   - Bank account details

2. APPLICATION PROCESS:
   a) Visit an Airtel Money business center
   b) Request the merchant/paybill application form
   c) Submit completed application with required documents
   d) Pay applicable fees

ADVANTAGES OF HAVING YOUR OWN PAYBILL:
-------------------------------------

1. BRAND OWNERSHIP:
   - The paybill will display "Hyrise Crown" instead of "KACH COMM SOLUTIONS"
   - Creates trust with your customers who see your actual business name

2. DIRECT COMMISSION MANAGEMENT:
   - All commissions will go directly to your account
   - No need to request transfers from a third party

3. CONTROL AND FLEXIBILITY:
   - Full access to transaction reports and data
   - Ability to integrate with your own systems
   - Can modify commission rates as needed

4. CUSTOMER CONFIDENCE:
   - Customers see your registered business name on receipts
   - Professional appearance builds trust

ESTIMATED TIMEFRAME:
-------------------
Application to approval: 5-7 business days
Integration testing: 1-2 days
Total implementation: 1-2 weeks

COSTS:
------
Application fee: KES 999 - 1,500 (one-time)
Monthly fee: KES 0 - 1,000 (varies based on transaction volume)

NEXT STEPS:
----------
1. Print the application form or visit a Safaricom/Airtel shop
2. Gather all required documentation
3. Submit application
4. Upon approval, update your systems with the new paybill number
5. Inform customers of your new paybill number

Note: The scripts have been pre-configured with paybill number 959595, which you can update to match the actual number you receive upon approval.
