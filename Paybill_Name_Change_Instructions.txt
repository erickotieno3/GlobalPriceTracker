INSTRUCTIONS FOR UPDATING PAYBILL BUSINESS NAME
----------------------------------------------

For M-PESA (Safaricom) in Kenya:

1. Visit a Safaricom Retail Shop or M-PESA Business Center
   - Bring original business registration documents
   - Bring ID of the authorized signatory
   - Bring proof of business address (utility bill)
   - Bring bank statements showing the connection to account 01521209171200

2. Fill out the "Business Name Change Form"
   - This form is provided at Safaricom shops
   - Note that you may need to pay a processing fee (around Ksh 1,000 to 2,000)

3. Submit a formal letter (use the template provided)
   - Make sure to keep a copy stamped by Safaricom as proof of submission

4. Follow-up procedure
   - Request a reference number for your application
   - Get contact details of the person handling your request
   - Business name changes typically take 5-7 business days to process

5. After approval
   - Conduct a test transaction to confirm the name change
   - Update all your marketing materials with the new business name
   - Inform your customers about the name change

For Airtel Money (if applicable):
Follow a similar process but visit an Airtel Money Business Center.

For Other Mobile Money Providers:
The process is generally similar but may have slight variations. Always call their customer service first to confirm requirements.

IMPORTANT NOTES:
---------------
1. Do not make any major changes to your account during this transition period.
2. Continue to monitor transfers to ensure they're correctly attributed to your business.
3. Consider creating a communication to your customers explaining the name change.
4. If the process takes longer than 10 business days, follow up daily.
5. Keep records of all communications with the mobile money provider.

If rejected, common reasons include:
- Incomplete documentation
- Discrepancies between documents
- Outstanding fees or charges
- Previous account issues

If your request is rejected, address the specific reason and resubmit.
