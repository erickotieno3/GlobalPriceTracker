# Google Play Store Listing Information

## App Information

### Basic Details
- **App Name**: Tesco Price Comparison
- **Short Description**: Compare supermarket prices globally across Tesco, Walmart, and more.
- **Full Description**: 
  Tesco Price Comparison is your global shopping companion that helps you find the best deals across supermarkets and chain stores worldwide. With real-time price tracking across Tesco, Walmart, Carrefour, Aldi, and more, you can save money on groceries and everyday essentials no matter where you are.

  **Key Features:**
  - Compare prices across multiple supermarkets in real-time
  - Coverage in multiple countries including UK, USA, Germany, Kenya, and more
  - Barcode scanning to quickly check prices while in-store
  - Price alerts when products you follow drop in price
  - Weekly deals and promotions from major retailers
  - Create shopping lists with best-price recommendations
  - Multi-language support (English, Swahili, French, German, Arabic)

  Save money on your shopping with Tesco Price Comparison - your trusted global price comparison tool.
