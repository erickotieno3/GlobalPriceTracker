1 Deploy Your App First

Before generating the Android package, you must deploy the app so PWABuilder can access it via a live URL.

Your live URL will be: https://hyrisecrown.com
Make sure DNS is pointing to your Replit deployment before proceeding.

Click "Publish" on the Replit Deploy button to get your app live, then proceed to step 2.

2 Generate Android APK via PWABuilder

PWABuilder is a free Microsoft tool that converts your web app into a ready-to-upload Android app bundle.

  1. Go to pwabuilder.com
  2. Enter your URL: https://hyrisecrown.com
  3. Click "Start" – it will analyse your manifest and service worker
  4. When the report shows, click "Package for Stores"
  5. Select "Google Play"
  6. Fill in:
    • Package ID: com.hyrisecrown.tescopricecomparison
    • App name: Hyrise Crown Price Compare
    • Version: 1.0.0 / Version code: 1
    • Signing: choose "Let PWABuilder handle signing" for easiest setup
  7. Click "Generate" – download the ZIP file
Open PWABuilder →

3 Update assetlinks.json with Your Signing Key

After generating the APK in PWABuilder, it will show you a SHA-256 certificate fingerprint. You need to add this to your app for Google to verify the domain.

Important: Copy the SHA-256 fingerprint from the PWABuilder download page and contact your developer to update public/.well-known/assetlinks.json with the real fingerprint before submitting to Play Store.

The file is already created at: hyrisecrown.com/.well-known/assetlinks.json

4 Submit to Google Play Console

  1. Go to play.google.com/console and sign in with erickotienokjv@gmail.com
  2. Click "Create app"
  3. Fill in app details:
    • App name: Hyrise Crown Price Compare
    • Default language: English
    • App type: App
    • Free / Paid: Free
  4. Go to Production → Create new release
  5. Upload the .aab file from your PWABuilder ZIP
  6. Fill in the release notes and click "Review release"
  7. Complete the store listing:
    • Short description: Compare supermarket prices globally – save money shopping smarter
    • Full description: (see below)
    • Category: Shopping
    • Screenshots: at least 2 phone screenshots
    • Feature graphic: 1024×500px banner image
  8. Submit for review – Google typically reviews in 3–7 days
Open Play Console →
